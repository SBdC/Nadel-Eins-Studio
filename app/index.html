About

The Nadel Eins Studio Berlin was founded way back in the 90ies by the band Jeans Team. This is where they recorded all their music and slowly developed their technical skills. After several moves and different setups throughout all those years, Franz Schütte decided to finally turn it into a real high-end music studio with the approach to create a room that sounds just beautiful.

It became a minimal but very verasatile plug and play work-station where musicians and technicians can easily addapt and find their way around. It works very well for all kind of audio purposes. Not only the classical recording, mixing and mastering.

This is a real time saver and a great reference!
